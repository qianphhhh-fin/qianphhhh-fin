### ğŸ¤– LLM-Driven Financial Agents & Market Microstructure
> *Keywords: Agent-RL, GRPO, Market Microstructure, Prospect Theory*

*   **Alignment via GRPO**: Utilized `TRL` framework and `GRPO` algorithm to fine-tune Qwen-0.6B/1.7B/4B models, effectively solving hallucination issues in numerical decision-making.
*   **Result**: Successfully reproduced nonlinear market characteristics such as liquidity runs and herding behavior.

### ğŸ“ˆ Macro-Narrative Factor Mining via LLMs
> *Keywords: Narrative Ambiguity, Robust Control, Qwen3-Embedding*
*   **Theoretical Innovation**: Introduced LLMs as cognitive agents under a **Robust Control** framework to quantify the "entropy" of market understanding regarding FOMC policies.
*   **Implementation**: Used Qwen3-Embedding to map text into high-dimensional semantic space and calculated Euclidean distance variance to construct the "Narrative Ambiguity Factor".
*   **Empirical Evidence**: Found that this factor carries a significant negative risk premium, orthogonal to traditional indicators like VIX or EPU.
